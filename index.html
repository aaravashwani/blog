Joe Biden

46th President of the United States.

Bio

Joe Biden is an American politician currently serving as the 46th president of the United States. He was first elected into office in 1972 and was a senator for 36 years. He also became Barack Obama's vice president from 2009 until 2017, when Donald Trump became the president. He ran for president three times, once in 1987 before dropping out, and once in 2008, when he dropped out again, and settled for Obama's vice president. He and his running mate, Kamala Harris won the election in 2020, making Joe Biden the United States' 46th president.
